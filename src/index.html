The method

Audit. Correct. Monitor.

01

Audit

We put 50–100 of the questions your prospects actually ask to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity — in English and Chinese — and document precisely where you stand, who is recommended instead, and every factual error these systems state about you. Delivered within a week.

02

Correct

We remediate the sources these systems rely on — structured data, directories, business profiles, review platforms, and your own pages — restructured the way AI reads them, in both languages, and strictly within medical-advertising and professional-conduct rules.

03

Monitor

These models change without notice. We re-measure your share of AI recommendations each month, flag material shifts, and hold your position as competitors begin to act — reported before and after, with evidence.

We don't game these systems. We make your practice genuinely worth citing — accurate, authoritative, and present wherever AI looks for an answer.
